Meet Aydın

Aydın Yelkovan is running for Executive Vice President of External Affairs to make UCSD affordable and accountable.

What I've worked on:

In the Bay Area

  • Students for Justice in Palestine — Event Coordinator at Diablo Valley College and Northern California SJP Coalition Constitution.
  • RepresentUs — Call Captain & Local Leader fighting for anti-corruption campaigns, fair elections, ranked-choice voting.
  • California Clean Money Campaign — Succesfully lobbied for the Fair Elections Act of 2026 onto the statewide ballot.

Since moving to San Diego

  • Students’ Civil Liberties Union — Led canvassing for sanctuary spaces across the city.
  • Disability Resource Hub — Fought for its establishment with the Blind Snakes Co-op and connected its leadership to disability studies scholars across the state.
  • UAW 4811 — Organized solidarity efforts between graduate student-workers in UAW 4811 and undergraduate student organizations to expand the Immigrant Legal Defense Fund and increase TA appointments.
  • Fought UCPD Surveillance — Worked with Ellis Collective to pressure UCSDPD to stop accepting DHS grants for AI surveillance. Convinced several professors to offer alternatives to invasive e-proctoring software.
  • SJP San Diego — Worked with SJP San Diego to pressure the university against hiring genocidal military strategist Eitan Shamir, who believes “the levelling of Gaza also poses an opportunity.”
  • Grassroots partners — Learned the history and current efforts of San Diego politics through engagement with local chapters of the Democratic Socialists of America, Alliance of Californians for Community Empowerment, along with the Tenants’ Council of San Diego, The Justice Workshop, and more local organizations.

Our vision

Invest in students, not against us.

Ethical investment

  • Adopt an Ethical Investment Policy to divest billions of dollars from harmful practices and free up funds for investments in students’ basic needs.

Affordability & equity

  • Free 2-hour student parking in every structure.
  • End parking enforcement ticketing quotas.
  • Affordable options in 24-hour market.
  • Expand emergency housing programs.

Accountability & democracy

  • End the admin veto on Associated Students.
  • Let students put policy on the ballot.
  • End ALL UCSDPD collaboration with ICE and DHS.

Campus experience

  • Make professors learn to teach.
  • End quarterly re-verification of disabilities.
