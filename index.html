Meet Aydın

Aydın Yelkovan is running for Executive Vice President of External Affairs to make UCSD affordable and accountable to students — free short-term parking, affordable food, 24-hour markets, more teaching assistants, and real pedagogy training for faculty.

What I've worked on:

In the Bay Area

  • Students for Justice in Palestine — Event Coordinator at Diablo Valley College; “Best Cultural Impact,” Northern California SJP Coalition Constitution.
  • RepresentUs — Call Captain & Local Leader; anti-corruption campaigns, fair elections, ranked-choice voting.
  • California Clean Money Campaign — Lobbyist for the Fair Elections Act of 2026 on the statewide ballot.

Since moving to San Diego

  • Students’ Civil Liberties Union — Led canvassing for sanctuary spaces across the city.
  • Disability Resource Hub — With Blind Snakes Co-Operative; tied to disability studies across the UC.
  • UAW 4811 — Solidarity to expand the Immigrant Legal Defense Fund and TA appointments.
  • Ellis Collective — Pressed UCSDPD on DHS surveillance grants; pushed alternatives to invasive e-proctoring.
  • SJP San Diego — Campaigned against hiring Eitan Shamir, who called “the levelling of Gaza” an “opportunity.”
  • Grassroots partners — DSA, ACCE, Tenants’ Council, Justice Workshop, and more.

Our vision

Invest in students, not against us.

Concrete priorities for an affordable, democratic UCSD — the same planks as the full platform, at a glance.

Ethical investment

  • Adopt an Ethical Investment Policy — divest from harmful holdings and free funds for student needs.

Affordability & equity

  • Free 2-hour student parking in every structure.
  • End parking enforcement ticketing quotas.
  • Affordable options in 24-hour markets; extend hours campus-wide.
  • Expand emergency housing programs.

Accountability & democracy

  • End the admin veto on Associated Students.
  • Let students put policy on the ballot.
  • UC Students’ Assembly — deliberate with leadership.
  • End UCSDPD collaboration with ICE and DHS.

Campus experience

  • Make professors learn to teach.
  • More TAs, better ratios.
  • End quarterly re-verification of disabilities.
